About Welfare Group of Bilaspur

The Welfare Group of Bilaspur is a dedicated community organization committed to creating positive change in the lives of underprivileged families. Founded with the vision of building a more compassionate society, we work tirelessly to address the basic needs of those who need support the most. Our organization believes in the power of collective action and community spirit to transform lives and create lasting impact. Through our various initiatives, we strive to bring hope, dignity, and essential resources to families across Bilaspur, ensuring that no one is left behind in our journey toward a better tomorrow.

Our Aim

We are a passionate group of Class 9 students from Bilaspur, united by a simple goal — to make life a little better for those in need.

Every week, we distribute food, clothes, and daily essentials to underprivileged families in our community. What began as a small act of kindness has grown into a movement of hope and compassion.

Our Mission

We focus on three key areas to create meaningful impact in our community

Education Support

Providing educational resources, school supplies, and learning materials to help children continue their studies and build a brighter future.

Health & Medical Aid

Supporting families with medical expenses, health awareness programs, and access to basic healthcare services for better community wellness.

Food

Distributing food packages, clothing, and daily essentials to ensure basic needs are met for underprivileged families in our community.
